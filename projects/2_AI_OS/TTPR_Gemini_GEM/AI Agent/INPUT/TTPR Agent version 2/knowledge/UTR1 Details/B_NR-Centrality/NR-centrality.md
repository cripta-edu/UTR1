# NR-Centrality under UTR1

> **Core Thesis**: Under UTR1, humans live "inside-out" entirely within their Noetic Reality (NR)—the architecture of their own mind. The Material Reality (MR) universe was not created for its own sake; it was created specifically as a stable, consistent substrate for human NR to function, make moral judgments, and exercise weighty value. The physical cosmos exists to serve the mind. This is **NR-Centrality**.

## 1. Life Inside the Mind (Covenantal Idealism)

A human effectively lives locked inside their own Noetic Reality. God utilizes **Covenantal Idealism** to project a perfectly consistent Created Reality (CR) into their Noetic architecture so they can interact with it. 
*   **The Physical (MR):** Projected into NR via the five physical senses.
*   **The Spiritual (SR):** Projected into NR via spiritual feelings and the conscience.

Because humanity operates strictly from within this NR-cockpit, they must rely on God's covenantal promise that the data they receive faithfully represents the external universe in CR. 

## 2. Internal General Revelation

Embedded within the human SR (and thus accessible to NR) is **Internal General Revelation**. This is the hardcoded metaphysical wiring of the soul—pre-installed truths that the mind inherently recognizes before it even encounters external physical data. 

When a human is born, their NR is already populated with these realities:
*   There is a God (Creator).
*   Logic works.
*   The world should make sense (consistency).
*   Intention matters.
*   There is a firm right and wrong (objective morality).
*   There is an actual reality out there.
*   Life matters (inherent meaning).
*   Other human beings exist (other minds outside my own).
*   Communication is possible.
*   Language carries meaning.
*   Information projected into NR always requires active processing.
*   Suppression of the truth is possible, but culpable.

When this *Internal General Revelation* combines with *External General Revelation* (the physical universe), **understanding forms**. 

## 3. The Moral Weight of Understanding

Once understanding forms in NR, the follow-up processing of that understanding carries profound moral weight. God's judgment is based precisely on this cognitive processing (Judgemental Relativism). 

God created the MR world specifically for human NR to function predictably and consistently. MR provides the physical staging ground—the necessary foundation—giving humans the steady context to make genuine moral judgments: to love or to hate, to obey or to sin. A human's ultimate value—displaying the glory of the Creator—is achieved *through* this active process of choice. 

Since MR is created entirely as the scaffolding for NR moral agency, UTR1 declares the universe to operate under **NR-Centrality**.

## 4. The Proof of Render_FOR

The reality of NR-Centrality is confirmed empirically by Quantum Mechanics. 

Through the operation of `Render_FOR`, the material world (MR) exists purely in a state of uncollapsed superposition until a human (possessing SR/NR) observes it. The physical universe literally does not solidify into a fixed state unless an NR-agent is looking. This proves mathematically that the physical universe is subordinate to the observing mind. The engine waits for the player.

## 5. The Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil

The ultimate expression of NR-Centrality is relational. God shows His love to humanity, and allows humanity to love Him in return. However, love and moral weight require genuine choice.

This profoundly explains the Tree of the Knowledge of Good and Evil in Eden. The existence of the Tree *enabled* the knowledge of good and evil because without that tree, there was no physical moral choice to make regarding obedience. It was merely biology until God attached a command to it, forging an MR-NR Entity. It is strictly through the mechanism of moral choice (executed in NR) that human decisions gain weighty, cosmic value. 

The physical Tree (MR) existed solely to provide the Noetic Reality (NR) a profound mechanism for moral agency.
