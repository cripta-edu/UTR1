# Superposition in Created Reality (CR)

However, the UTR1 framework introduces the concept of **Superpositional Realism**, arguing that genuine superposition—uncollapsed states of potential—must exist within the actual substrate of Created Reality (CR), specifically Material Reality (MR), before God executes a Rendering event.

This initially appears to conflict with orthodox Divine Sovereignty (implying Open Theism or Synergism). Nevertheless, placing superposition directly into CR is critical for the UTR1 architecture for two distinct, load-bearing reasons: Quantum Mechanics and the Ontology of Prayer.

David escapes, collapsing the wave function into a different reality. The potential of surrender was a genuine superposition (a valid "could-be") in CR, fully known by UR.
