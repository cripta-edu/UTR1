# Retrocausality under UTR1

## 1. The Secular Confusion
In secular quantum mechanics, phenomena like the *Delayed-Choice Quantum Eraser* experiment suggest that an observation made in the present can retroactively dictate the path a particle took in the past. 
*   **The Materialist Crisis**: Because materialism assumes a closed, linear, "flat reality" devoid of a conscious overarching observer, retrocausality appears absurd. It suggests time itself is broken. 
*   **Failed Patches**: To avoid this, secular science often retreats into the "Many-Worlds" interpretation (creating infinite meaningless universes to avoid collapse entirely).

To our linear NR, it *looks* as if the present observation reached back and altered the past. In reality, the past was simply in superposition until God rendered the entire chain of events simultaneously.
