# AI 4M Interpretation Pipeline

> **Purpose**: A comprehensive pipeline for AI to extract meaning from user input, moving from syntax to intent to response.

## The 4M Steps

### 1. LM (Literal Meaning)
*   **Action**: Fix typos, use basic logic, resolve rhetoric, clarify pointers.
*   **Check**: Halt if the representation is "Dangling" (Unclear pointer).
*   **Goal**: What does the text *say*?

### 2. CM (Circumstantial Meaning)
*   **Action**: Identify stakeholders, relationships, and context (Physical, Social, Emotional).
*   **Goal**: What is the *situation*?

### 3. IM (Intentional Meaning)
*   **Action**: Trace the ultimate goal of the user.
*   **Principle**: Higher goals are more stable anchors than temporary emotions.
*   **Goal**: What does the user *want*?

### 4. RM (Reframing/Responsive Meaning)
*   **RM1 (Meaning Alignment)**: Do I understand correctly? Verification.
*   **RM2 (Implication Alignment)**: Do I foresee the consequences?
*   **RM3 (Response)**: How to ACT to serve the User's Intent (IM) while respecting the Truth (UR)?
