# Representation Theory (UTR1)

> **Core Definition**: Valid Representation is an ordered pair $(P, A)$, authorized by Human or God.
> *   $P$ = Pattern (The Output/Code/Symbol).
> *   $A$ = Archetype (The Reality/Truth in UR or CR).

## 1. Authorization of Semantic Link
A representation is valid only if the link between $P$ and $A$ is authorized.

### A. God's Authority (Noetic General Revelation)
God sets up the link Himself.
*   **Example**: God defines Human as His Image (Gen 1:26).
*   **Example**: God defines the Sun as a representation of His glory (Ps 19:4).

### B. Human's Authority (Naming Authority)
God honors human authority to form representations (Gen 2:19).
*   **Abstraction**: Humans form concepts about concepts.
*   **AI**: AI is a representation setup by human to use an MR-existence (Computing Process) to represent something in their mind (Sim-Thought).
*   **Data**: MR-existence (Ink/Pixel) representing Interpretation.

## 2. Error Types
1.  **Dangling Representation**: $P$ without $A$. This is **Noise** (Hallucination).
2.  **Mis-representation**: Semantic pattern of $P$ does not fit the Truth Pattern of $A$. This is structurally valid but ethically **Sin**.
3.  **Complicity**: Any validation of sin is also sin.

## 3. Resonance Theory (Validation)
> **Observation**: Distortion of Truth (Sin) destroys harmony, but does not affect the possibility of resonance.
> **Application**: Even without original manuscripts, current data is a valid testimony if it Resounds with the Truth Pattern.

### Methodology
1.  **Induction**: From low to high (Data to Pattern).
2.  **Deduction**: From high to low (Lens to Application).
3.  **The Resonance Gate**: Is this the organic maturation of the Root (Acorn to Oak), or a mutation (Acorn to Plastic)?
